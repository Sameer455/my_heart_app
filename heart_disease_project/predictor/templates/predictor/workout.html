🏋️‍♂️ Heart-Healthy Workout Plan 🏋️‍♀️

Improve heart health with this structured exercise routine.

💪 Beginner Level (30 min)

✅ 10 min Brisk Walking 🚶‍♂️ ✅ 10 min Bodyweight Squats & Lunges 🏋️‍♂️ ✅ 10 min Stretching & Cool Down 🧘‍♀️

🔥 Intermediate Level (45 min)

✅ 15 min Jogging or Cycling 🚴‍♂️ ✅ 15 min Resistance Training (Push-ups, Squats) 💪 ✅ 15 min Core & Balance Exercises (Planks, Yoga) 🧘‍♂️

🏆 Advanced Level (60 min)

✅ 20 min High-Intensity Cardio (Jump Rope, Sprints) 🏃‍♂️ ✅ 20 min Strength Training (Dumbbells, Kettlebells) 🏋️‍♀️ ✅ 20 min Recovery (Stretching & Meditation) 🧘‍♀️

💡 Additional Tips

✅ Exercise at least **5 days a week** 🗓️ ✅ Stay **hydrated** & eat a **balanced diet** 💧🥗 ✅ Listen to your body & avoid overexertion 🚑
